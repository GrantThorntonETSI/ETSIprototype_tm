Response to Office Action The examining attorney is the person most familiar with your application and can answer specific questions about your office action. The examining attorney’s name and contact information are found at the end of your office action. Before you contact the examining attorney, read the office action carefully. If you can’t reach the examining attorney immediately, wait at least two business days for him or her to respond. If the attorney is out of the office, contact his or her supervisor. To find supervisor contact information, go to the trademark contact information webpage and find the senior attorney for your examining attorney’s particular law office. The law office number is listed in the examining attorney’s signature block at the end of the office action.
