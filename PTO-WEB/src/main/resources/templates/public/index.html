The USPTO is working to gradually replace many of the credentials you use to sign in to our systems with one secure, consolidated sign-in: your new uspto.gov account. The uspto.gov account has already combined sign-in credentials for several fee payment tools and we’re working on consolidating even more. Get started today. Create your uspto.gov account and start customizing your MyUSPTO page.

The new uspto.gov accounts are designed for individuals, not groups or organizations. Future updates will add the ability for organizations to share information between colleagues.
